Why Cape Town?

Cape Town, The Mother City

Cape Town, city and seaport, legislative capital of South Africa and capital of Western Cape province. The city lies at the northern end of the Cape Peninsula some 30 miles (50 kilometres), at its southernmost boundary, north of the Cape of Good Hope. Because it was the site of the first European settlement in South Africa, Cape Town is known as the country’s “mother city.”

Today Cape Town is a modern city with high-rise office buildings and pedestrian malls. Although it is a major political and economic centre, its reputation still rests on its beautiful situation between mountain and sea, its cosmopolitan population, and the liberal outlook of many of its citizens.

My Favourite Cafes in Cape Town

Nourish’d Café

Nourish'd is a plant-based, zero waste cafe and juicery.

Address

177 Kloof St, Gardens, Cape Town, 8001

What I like about it

It’s the perfect care-free escape from the hustle and bustle of the Mother City. And, the fact that every dish served looks like a work of art and their décor is a bohemian breath of fresh air means you’ll get some seriously cool Insta-worthy snapshots of your Nourish’d experience. All while indulging in their wide variety of menu options.

Clarke’s Bar & Dining Room

Lively restaurant and night-time party spot with burger-led menu.

Address

133 Bree St, Cape Town City Centre, Cape Town, 8001

What I like about it

Besides it’s cool laid-back café vibe and trendy Bree Street location. Clarke’s has become well known for their incredible all-day breakfasts, delicious burgers, sensational cocktails and drinks selection and flavour-filled menu options. Ensuring that there's something for everyone to enjoy!

Origin Coffee Roasting Café

Origin is also one of South Africa’s first artisan coffee roasters

Address

28 Hudson Street De Waterkant, Cape Town

What I like about it

Situated in a historic brickhouse and courtyard. Origin’s location is every bit as unique as it’s incredible cups of coffee. In addition to their sensational cups of brew, Origin has an exciting menu packed with baked-goods, gluten-free and vegan options, tasty plates and bowls and breakfast options.
